How it works

Powered by AI, Gagali engages with you in an interactive journal to ensure therapy continuity between sessions. Without the fear of judgement, express your true self and pave the way for a more effective therapy.

  • During sessions, Gagali listens, transcribes and writes medical notes
  • Between sessions, Gagali engages with patient in interactive journals to ensure continuty of care
  • Therapist has access to the patient's entries along with summaries and insights.
